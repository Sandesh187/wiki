Project Overview

Background

Plastic is widely used because it is cheap, lightweight, and durable. However, these same properties also make plastic difficult to break down naturally. As a result, plastic waste accumulates in the environment and causes long-term pollution.

Motivation

We chose this project because plastic pollution is a visible problem in our surroundings. By working on this topic, we wanted to better understand how science and biology can contribute to environmental protection.

Project Objectives

  • Study the impact of plastic waste on the environment
  • Learn about biological methods used in waste management
  • Understand how enzymes can help in plastic breakdown
  • Document our learning through a structured wiki website

Scope of the Project

This project is mainly focused on learning and understanding concepts rather than building a full-scale system. The work presented here represents a student-level study and literature-based understanding of how biological approaches may help in plastic waste management.

Expected Outcomes

By completing this project, we expect to gain a better understanding of environmental biotechnology, improve our research and documentation skills, and learn how to present technical topics in a simple and organized manner.
