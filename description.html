Project Description

Problem Statement

Plastic waste is difficult to recycle completely. Many plastics end up in rivers and oceans, harming the environment.

Our Idea

We studied how certain bacteria and enzymes can help in breaking plastic into smaller, less harmful parts.

Basic Approach

  • Study plastic types
  • Learn about plastic-degrading enzymes
  • Understand how bacteria grow
  • Analyze simple data and graphs
