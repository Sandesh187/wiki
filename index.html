Plastic Waste Reduction Project

This website presents our student project focused on understanding plastic pollution and exploring how biological methods can help reduce plastic waste.

About the Project

Plastic waste is a major environmental issue across the world. Large amounts of plastic are dumped into landfills, rivers, and oceans, where it takes hundreds of years to degrade. Our project studies how biology can be used as a possible tool to reduce this problem.

Project Aim

The main aim of our project is to understand how enzymes and bacteria can help in breaking down certain types of plastic into smaller components that are easier to manage or recycle.

What We Learned

  • Basics of plastic pollution and its effects
  • Introduction to enzymes and how they work
  • How biology can be applied to environmental problems
  • How to create and organize a simple project website

Why This Website

This wiki was created as part of an academic assignment to document our project work, organize our ideas, and present our learning in a structured and clear way.
